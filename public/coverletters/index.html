Guidelines for Cover Letters

Neuroanatomy and Behaviour does not require extensive cover letters and uploading a separate file as a cover letter is not required. A brief 1-2 description to introduce your paper is sufficient - we will read your abstract and look at your whole paper before deciding whether to send it out for review.
